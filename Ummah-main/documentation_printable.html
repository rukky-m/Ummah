Ummah Cooperative Digital Secretariate

Official System Documentation & Resource Guide

🌟 Introduction

Think of this application as a Digital Secretariate. Just as a physical office has files, clerks, and a manager, this system organizes all cooperative activities—savings, loans, and accounting—into a neat, digital space.

🏗️ 1. How the System Works (The "House" Analogy)

To understand the technology (Laravel) behind this, imagine we are building a house:

  • The Blueprint (Models): These are like the official forms (Member profiles, Loan requests). They define what information we keep.
  • The Clerk (Controllers): These are the workers. When you click a button, the Clerk takes your request, checks the rules, and updates the files.
  • The Office Rooms (Views): This is what you see on your screen. The dashboard and application pages are the "rooms" where you interact with the Clerk.
  • The Receptionist (Routes): This is the person at the front door who directs you. If you say "I want to see my savings," the Receptionist sends you to the Savings Room.

🏦 2. Key Features for Members

📝 Joining the Cooperative

The registration is a step-by-step process: personal details, next of kin, and final review. Once submitted, a Staff member must approve your application before you can start saving.

💰 Savings & Contributions

Personal Savings: This is your private wallet within the cooperative. You can deposit and withdraw as needed.

Contributions: Fixed monthly amounts that everyone pays. This is the collective "fuel" that allows the cooperative to lend and grow.

💸 Loans

Applying for a loan is a secure process involving a formal request, two guarantors who vouch for you, and a review by the Loan Committee to ensure safety and fairness.

⚙️ 3. The "Brain" (The Math & Logic)

The system follows strict predefined rules to ensure transparency:

📈 Interest Rates

The computer automatically calculates interest based on the loan's purpose:

  • Emergency & Essential Goods: Attracts a 6% interest rate.
  • Business or Other Needs: Attracts a 10% interest rate.

🧮 Balance Calculations

Total Deposits - Total Withdrawals = Current Balance.

The system calculates balances from the full transaction history every time. This ensures the history remains the absolute truth even if a single record is edited.

📖 4. The Admin Control Room

📓 The Cashbook

This is the Master Ledger. It tracks every single Naira that enters or leaves the cooperative, including staff expenses and utility costs.

🛡️ Security & Roles

The system uses "Role-Based Access." Members can only see their own data, Staff handle operations, and Admins oversee the entire organization's health.
